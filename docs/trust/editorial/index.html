Editorial Policy

Every research-facing claim must be evidence-linked to arXiv.

Quality gates (enforced in CI)

  • Every <Claim> block must include at least one arXiv ID.
  • Tendencies must list >=2 foundational, >=2 new-wave, and >=1 debate paper.
  • Overall citation coverage must remain >=95%.
  • Tools and benchmarks must include evidence arrays.
  • arXiv IDs are validated against local snapshots and anchors.

Corrections & ledgering

Corrections are logged publicly and linked to the evidence update that resolved them.

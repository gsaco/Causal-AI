Editorial Policy

Every research-facing claim must be evidence-linked to arXiv.

Claim requirements

  • All claims include at least one arXiv ID.
  • Non-arXiv sources are labeled as secondary supporting sources.
  • When evidence is incomplete, content is marked as a hypothesis.

Corrections

Corrections are logged publicly and linked to the evidence update that resolved them.

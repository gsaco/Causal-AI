Biology, Genomics, and Drug Discovery - Applications

Causal reasoning for experimental design, target discovery, and intervention effects.

application Biology, Genomics, and Drug Discovery

The causal question

Which interventions change biological outcomes, and how can we design experiments that identify causal mechanisms?

Common failure modes without causality

  • Confounding between measurement processes and biological signal.
  • Distribution shift across labs or assays.
  • Counterfactuals are claimed without causal identification.

Backdoor Criterion

Evidence (1)
2002.02770

A graphical condition that blocks all backdoor paths from treatment to outcome using observed covariates.

CATE

Evidence (1)
1705.08821

Conditional Average Treatment Effect, the expected effect of treatment conditioned on covariates.
